ABOUT

Mama P's Cafe started in 2018 by our very own Herron design students.Located in the heart of downtown - walking distance from Herron School of Art and Design. Mama P's Cafe started as a need for Herron students to have food choices close to Eskanazi Hall. Well, after years of eating from the vending machine, Visual Communication Design students put their efforts together adn came up with Mama P's Cafe.

In addition to our full espresso and brew bar menu, we serve fresh, comforting apple pie made by Mama P herself.
